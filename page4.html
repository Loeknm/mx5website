The Fourth Generation

The "ND" generation is the 4th and last (for now). Its a new, small roadster in a world full of electric cars and SUV's, but that doesn't mean that the MX-5 wont fit in. It remains a beacon of affordable fun. But now with all the new technology that you would want in a car.

Mazda MX5 ND RF

In late 2016 a new model of the ND was announced: the MX-5 RF (retractable fastback) which did not change the car performance wise, but it did change the visuals a lot! The car now offers a electric powered retractable hardtop roof that can convert your little sports car in just 13 seconds. It also gives the car a coupe-like look to it. .

Fuel consumption

In these modern times fuel consumption is becoming more important by the day, not just for your wallet but for the planet too. That is why Mazda focused on keeping the car as light as possible, with the lightest model being only 960kg! Combined with a 1.5 L engine on the base model results in 6.0l/100 km which is below the average fuel consumption in the Nederlands, says Autoweek which is pretty impressive for a sports car.
