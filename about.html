About MX-5 Automotive

MX-5 Automotive is run by one person that is really interested in cars, especially japanese cars. And especially.. The Mazda MX5-5.

Why a MX-5?

I've always thought that the MX-5 was one of the most influencial sport cars ever, its price and reliability made it widely available to any person. And while i like big engines and big cars, i think its cooler that a car can go this fast with so little power, i find it smart. My first car is a MX-5 NB, and while it has its problems i think its one of the best sportscars to buy, it has such a raw mechanical feeling to it that every weekend drive keeps bringing me joy, so see this website as a tribute to the MX-5
